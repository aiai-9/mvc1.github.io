Audio Samples from "MambaVoiceCloning: Efficient and Expressive Text-to-Speech via State-Space Modeling and Diffusion Control"

Abstract: This paper presents MambaVoiceCloning (MVC), a scalable and expressive text-to-speech (TTS) framework that unifies state-space sequence modeling with diffusion-driven style control. Distinct from prior diffusion-based models, MVC replaces all self-attention and recurrent components in the TTS pipeline with novel Mamba-based modules: a Bi-Mamba Text Encoder, Temporal Bi-Mamba Encoder, and Expressive Mamba Predictor.
